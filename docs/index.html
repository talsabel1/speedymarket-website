SpeedyMarket® is the ultimate solution for a streamlined and efficient grocery store experience for customers, managers and suppliers alike.

Say goodbye to long lines and complicated store navigation!

  • End-to-end shopping: manage lists, navigate, scan, and pay
  • Easy inventory management and order tracking
  • Streamlined supply chain from supplier to customer

Participating supermarkets use a QR code to be scanned on entrance to manage the shopping experience. Customers scan barcodes to add items to cart. The SpeedyMarket® application serves as a portal for all three types of users: customer, manager, supplier.

Features

SpeedyMarket® offers a comprehensive suite of features designed to enhance the shopping experience for customers, streamline store operations for managers, and simplify inventory management for suppliers.

Customer Portal

Barcode Scanning

Scan supermarket QR code on entrance to begin shopping. Easily add items to your cart by scanning product barcodes, making your shopping experience faster and more efficient.

In-Store Navigation

Smart navigation using graph algorithms to find the shortest path through the store based on your shopping list.

Secure Authentication

User data is protected with hashed passwords stored in the database, while tokens are securely saved in cookies for session management.

AI-Powered List Tools

Items can be extracted from handwritten shopping lists or recipe URLs and added to your shopping list, for convenience and efficiency.

Seamless Payments

Complete your checkout effortlessly with integrated Stripe Payment for secure transactions.

Manager Portal

Store Map Management

Build and manage the store's layout, including shelves and item locations, to ensure efficient in-store navigation for customers.

Inventory Tracking

Easily manage stock and product details, including real-time updates, ensuring the store operates efficiently.

Secure Authentication

User data is protected with hashed passwords stored in the database, while tokens are securely saved in cookies for session management.

Supplier Portal

Order Fulfillment

Manage and track orders sent from the store managers, ensuring timely deliveries to maintain inventory levels.

Services

SpeedyMarket® is powered by cutting-edge cloud and AI technologies for seamless shopping and optimized performance

Azure SQL Database

Reliable and scalable database management for secure data storage and fast retrieval.

SignalR

Real-time communication to synchronize user interactions across devices, for up-to-date inventory management. When an item is added to a cart, inventory and out-of-stock items are updated in real-time and reflected on all devices.

Azure Cloud Functions

Http triggered functions providing serverless computing for efficient execution of backend operations and business logic.

Computer Vision

Leveraging advanced image-to-text capabilities to convert handwritten shopping lists into digital format, allowing users to upload written lists directly into the app.

OpenAI API

Utilizing natural language processing to extract ingredients from online recipe URLs and automatically add them to the user's shopping list.

Azure Maps

Translating addresses to coordinate points in order to present supermarket locations on the map.

Google Maps

Integration with Google Maps for locating participating supermarkets nearby.

Stripe Payment

Incorporating Stripe Payment services for secure, streamlined payment processing, enhancing the overall shopping experience.

Country State City API

Provides accurate location data for selecting countries, states, and cities for supermarket location in management settings.
